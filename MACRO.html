TAPE SETS
配件一＿磁力收納膠帶架 /
在每個紙膠帶中安裝含有磁鐵的軸，運用磁力快速、便利且人性化的特性，讓紙膠帶可以更方便收納，從此之後紙膠帶再也不會散落各處。並透過不同配色的膠帶台來為不同款式、花色的紙膠帶作分類，讓擁有大量紙膠帶的使用者可以從數十捆中快速找到自己要用的膠帶。

A shaft containing a magnet is installed in each paper tape, and the use of magnetic force is fast, convenient and user-friendly, so that the paper tape can be stored more conveniently, and the paper tape will never be scattered everywhere. And through the tape table with different colors to classify different styles and colors of paper tape, so that users with a large number of paper tapes can quickly find the tape they want to use from dozens of bundles.

Paper tape itself is very good to tear, so in the design to collect the main axis for development, but also hope that the integrity of our products is higher, so we also have the design of magnetic tape cutters to make it easy for users to cut out beautiful edges, so that paper tape on the hand account can be more refined.
