DESCRIPTION​

KOOBA是一款磁力互動益智玩具，主要針對 3-6 歲的孩童做啟蒙設計。利用『 蒙特梭利教育理論 』，配合不同階段的孩童，養成能夠自我表達、認知的能力，並運用磁力方便、快速的特性，突破空間與結構的約束性，創造更多堆疊的方式，啟發孩童創新的思維。最後結合『 多元智能理論 』中的空間視覺、數學邏輯、音樂，以創新的設計改良傳統七巧板。

KOOBA is the magnetic tangram interactive educational toy. The biggest feature is through magnetic assistance, it's different from the average of the traditional tangram, let children step into the field of stereoscopic space, and combined with AR and other didital software, so that children in a short period of time to learn, and have good parent-child interaction with their families.




CASE STUDY
【 1897 Montessori Education x 1983 Multiple Intelligences Theory 】

在“蒙特梭利教育理論”中，孩子會經歷學習慾望與好奇心的階段，稱為「敏感期」。敏感期的孩童對周遭環境的特定事物會全神貫注，是身心發展的黃金時期。藉由這段時間去訓練兒童的獨立性，以及自我實現的能力，並結合加德納提出的“多元智能理論”，使兒童在各個階段都能接觸到合適的智能，進而完成全方位的發展。

In Montessori Educational Theory, children go through a stage of desire and curiosity called the "sensitive period". Children in the sensitive period pay full attention to specific things in their surroundings and are a golden period for physical and mental development. Through this time, children are trained in independence and self-actualization, Combined with Gardner's "multiple intelligence theory", children can be exposed to appropriate intelligence at all stages, and then complete all-round development.


​Trend Research | Competitive Product Analysis | Concept Ideas | User Positioning

在設計前期我帶領團隊利用TRELLO這個軟題並搜集大量的資料，資料中看到越來越多的產業的產品導入磁鐵，發現磁吸式產品的趨勢提升。因此我們以磁吸式​玩具作為我們產品的設定，並找了許多相關的競品做分析及研究，最終定位我們產品的方向及使用者。
